Medicare Supplement Questions

33 answered questions about Medigap and Medicare Supplement plans.

Medigap (Medicare Supplement) insurance fills the gaps Original Medicare leaves behind — copays, coinsurance, and deductibles. But the enrollment windows are strict, the premium trends vary, and plans are standardized differently by state. These questions cover everything from which plan to choose to what happens if you miss open enrollment.
