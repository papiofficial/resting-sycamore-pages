Medicare Part D Questions

24 answered questions about Medicare prescription drug coverage.

Part D drug coverage is often the most confusing piece of Medicare. Formularies change every year, the coverage gap (donut hole) still affects some enrollees, and low-income subsidy programs can dramatically reduce what you pay. These questions cover how Part D actually works and how to find the right plan for your specific medications.
